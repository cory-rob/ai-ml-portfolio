# Chatbot Sample Conversation

## User
I need help resetting my password.

## Bot
Sure, I can help with that. Please click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the reset instructions sent to your email.

## User
I did not receive the email.

## Bot
Please check your spam or junk folder first. If it is not there, confirm the email address linked to your account and try again.

## User
I still cannot access my account.

## Bot
I’m sorry about that. Please contact support with your registered email and a brief description of the issue for further assistance.
